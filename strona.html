Scientific Publishing House (SPH) was established in 2008 by the Centre of Sociological Research (CSR). Initially it was created for publishing scientific outcomes of the CSR team. However, in a short time it became a platform for scientist from Commonwealth of the Independent States and EU countries. And not just a platform for publishing, referencing, reading and citing their works, CSR team pays a lot of attention to human relations and makes everything possible to ensure close interaction of all authors and the editorial board for exchange of experience and information. We are a small and ambitious publishing house. At the present moment we are focused on publishing scientific literature from all fields of Economy and Sociology. We publish scientific books and journals such as Economics & Sociology, Journal of International Studies and special editions focused on the cutting-edge topics. However, we are open to communication and will be happy to bring to life your ideas using our experience and facilities.
